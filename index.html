What is Gimkit?

Gimkit is an educational game for students who want to learn while having fun! It shares various similarities with "Kahoot!", "Blooket", and "Quizizz". Josh Feinsilber, the founder and creator of Gimkit, hopes to create a practical, but fun learning environment for children around the world. Unlike "Kahoot!", "Blooket", and "Quizizz" Gimkit offers a much wider variety of gamemodes to choose among as well as more control over them. From snowball fights, to fishing, to even a recreation of Among Us, Gimkit is the way to go! Make a free account today or even purchase the exclusive $5 a month, Gimkit Pro extension which allows over five people to play on any of the Gimkit Pro Exclusive gamemodes. No mater, you can still play them for free, but only with amaximum of five players. That's why Gimkit Pro is preffered mainly for teachers with large classes. Although, that doesn't mean you can't still get it to play with your friends in order to increase the beneficial learning joy!
