What are all the gamemodes?

  • Classic: a Kahoot!-style gamemode

  • Teams: Classic but with pairs of allies

  • Super Rich: Classic but with WAY MORE money

  • Boss Battle: One person vs everyone else

  • Hidden: Player names and balances are hidden till the very end

  • Humans vs Zombies: One half vs the other

  • Drained: Real life concept of money dwindling

  • Draw It: A drawing-based game where the most points wins

  • Floor is Lava: Work together to stay above the rising lava and see how long you survive

  • Trust No One: An Among Us-inspired gamemode

  • What Is...: A jeapordy-styled gamemode

  • Infinity: Collect all six infinity stones or try to be part of the half of people that survive the snap

  • Fishtopia: A fishing game where you sit back and relax

  • Tag Domination: An epic game of tag between echo and vortex

  • Capture the Flag: Echo and vortex's rivalry continues in this epic game of capture the flag

  • This or That: A would-you-rather-styled game meant to help people learn more about each other while also having a little of bit fun in the process

  • One Way Out: A player vs environment type gamemode requiring teamwork to get everybody off the plant-infested ship safe and sound

  • Snowbrawl: An epic snowball fight where you but special gadgets to knock out your rivals

  • Snowy Survival: The corruption is spreading, how long will you survive

  • Farmchain: A farming gamemode where you must use cooperation and teamwork to your own advantage or have the most cash by the end depending on the gameplay type

Frostbite: Special Event

A special one-time event across all of Gimkit's 2d gamemodes.
