What is Gimkit Creative?

Gimkit Creative is the biggest and most recent update that Gimkit has ever had! Now, students are able to create their own gamemodes, their own maps, their own worlds! As of the 2022-2023 school year, everyone has three slots to use for their maps. Not to mention you don't even need Gimkit Pro to use this feature. Even with a free account, whether it's a teacher account or student account, the feature is still available on the left side of your screen. There will be a free hands-on tutorial which will teach you how to handle this amazing feature.

Gimkit's Lore: A Brief Overview

Hidden across the 2d gamemodes are secrets containing parts of the lore behind Gimkit. Mysterious figures such as The Crafter exist somewhere in the multiverse.
